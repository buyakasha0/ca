How can you Help?

Helping out can come in many different ways, but importantly it is your activity and engagement that makes the difference in addressing the issue. It showcases the rights and ensures that people's rights are heard and are made aware. The smallest steps can help encourage and inspire others to support the cause and make a change in their personal lives as well as the community around them.

Develop projects surrounding this SDG

One of the biggest ways to help personally is to incorporate the SDG into your personal, work or academic life to help raise awareness but also inspire others.

Talks & Workshops

Attending a talk or workshop can really help with informing others and yourself to show the situation of the SDG through testimony of others who have volunteered or have been impacted through this SDG.

Volunteer

Volunteering is one of the most impactful ways to help with any issue, it showcases your interest and moral standing without any return. This selfless act can really show the real consequences these SDGs can have on our personal lives.
